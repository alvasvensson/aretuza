Former students

At Aretuza, we are very proud of our former students

Yennefer of Vengerberg

Yennefer graduated from Aretuza at 13 years old. She fought in the battle of Sodden Hill and shortly after she joined the council of Wizards. She is the youngest sorcerer to join the council within the Brotherhood of Sorcerers.

Triss Merigold

After graduating from Aretuza, Triss Merigold served King Foltest of Temeria, and with help from the famous Geralt of Rivia saved the temerian Princess from her curse. She also fought the battle of Sodden Hill.

Keira Metz

After graduating from Aretuza, Keira Metz served as advisor to King Foltest of Temeria.

Sabrina Glevissig

After graduating from Aretuza, Sabrina Glevissig served as advisor to King Henselt of Kaedwen. She also fought in the battle of Sodden Hill.
